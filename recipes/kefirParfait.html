Kefir Parfait

Description:

This is a very simple and healthy recipe that requires minimal ingredients!

Feel free to substitute these frozen fruits with different frozen or fresh fruits.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup kefir
  • 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 cup of frozen mango
  • 2 Tablespoons honey

Directions:

  1. Add kefir to bowl.
  2. Add frozen fruits into the bowl.
  3. Drizzle honey into the bowl.
  4. Let the fruits thaw for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Mix contents of bowl and serve!
