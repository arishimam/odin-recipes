Avocado Toast

Description:

This is a very simple and healthy recipe that requires minimal ingredients!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Avocado
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 1/2 a Lime
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cilantro (Optional)
  • Chili Flakes (Optional)

Directions:

  1. Toast bread
  2. Peel the avocado, remove seed, and place avocado into a medium sized bowl.
  3. Squeeze lime juice into the bowl.
  4. Roughly chop cilantro and place 3/4 in bowl.
  5. Add salt and pepper.
  6. Use fork to mash and mix ingredients together. Continue until desired texture is reached.
  7. Put avocado mixture onto the bread.
  8. Garnish with remaining cilantro and chili flakes.
